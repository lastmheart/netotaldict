Swiss quality
infused with Russian soul

The balls, festivals, galas, and other events that make up Russian Nights in Switzerland are the building blocks we use to build a sturdy cultural bridge between Switzerland and Russia.

We preserve and pass on traditions, fill life with pleasures and emotions, promote development and evolution in business. And all that happens in a life-affirming atmosphere of a festivity.

Zlata Smirnova, founder

Our events are for successful and extraordinary people. Together with us, create event holidays that will be remembered for a lifetime, introduce friends, colleagues, business partners from Switzerland and other countries to the richness of Russian culture. After all, a festive atmosphere strengthens and develops the most valuable thing: relationships between people.
